Global Onboarding Program Architecture

End-to-end onboarding system for 1,000+ employees across 3 regions

Regions: US, India, EMEA
Role Tracks: 4 (Commercial, CS, Engineering, Corp)
Timeline: Pre-boarding through Day 90

Program Overview

Problem: Onboarding was owned by no one. HRIS triggered the offer, but what happened between offer acceptance and productive team member was a black box. New hires got lost. Managers did not know what they owned. Systems did not talk to each other.

Solution: Built a full onboarding operating system that connects HRIS, LMS, manager workflows, and team integration. Four role-specific tracks. Pre-boarding through 90-day milestones. Every handoff accounted for. Every gap closed.

What this shows: Systems thinking at scale. Building infrastructure that does not need constant fixing. Making invisible work visible.

Four Phases (All Tracks)
Phase 1
Pre-boarding
Offer acceptance to Day 1
  • Welcome email from manager (template provided)
  • Equipment order triggered via HRIS integration
  • LMS account created, pre-work assigned
  • Slack invite sent 3 days before start
  • Calendar holds for Week 1 sessions
Phase 2
Week 1
Days 1 to 5
  • Day 1: Company orientation (live, all new hires)
  • Days 1-2: Systems setup and access
  • Days 2-3: Department overview sessions
  • Days 3-5: Role-specific deep dive (LMS modules)
  • End of Week 1: Manager 1:1 check-in
Phase 3
Days 6 to 30
First month ramp
  • Complete role track certification
  • Shadow team members (role-specific targets)
  • First project or assignment assigned
  • Weekly manager 1:1s with structured agenda
  • Day 30: First milestone review
Phase 4
Days 31 to 90
Integration period
  • Transition from shadowing to leading
  • First major deliverable or quota responsibility
  • Peer mentor assigned for ongoing support
  • Day 60: Mid-point check-in with manager
  • Day 90: Onboarding completion survey + review
System Integrations

Connected Infrastructure

HRIS (Workday)

Triggers onboarding workflow on offer acceptance. Automates equipment orders, access requests, and manager notifications. Single source of truth for start dates and role assignments.

LMS (MindTickle)

Auto-enrolls new hires in role-specific tracks. Tracks completion milestones. Sends reminders to managers for check-ins at Days 7, 14, 30, 60, 90.

Slack

Auto-invites to company and department channels. Welcome bot sends intro messages. Manager receives notification with onboarding checklist on Day 1.

Salesforce

New Commercial and CS hires added to relevant territories and accounts. Training environment access granted Day 1. Live account access granted after certification.

Confluence

Onboarding hub with role-specific pages. Manager checklist templates. Team integration resources. Self-serve FAQ for common questions.

Calendar

Auto-books Week 1 orientation sessions. Sends manager reminders for scheduled 1:1s. Pre-loads team meetings and recurring syncs.
